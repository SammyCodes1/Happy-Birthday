Words From Those Who Know Her Best

Love letters, written in truth.

“

Temilade

Best friend since 2008
Omhonria Pauline, you have never once loved me halfway. You have sat beside me in silence, celebrated me without envy, and reminded me who I was when life made me forget. Growing up beside you has been one of the great privileges of my life.
“

Chisom

Older sister & first friend
You were the little girl who followed me everywhere, then became the woman I call when I need courage. Your tenderness is never weakness; it is the strongest thing in every room. I am endlessly proud to be your sister.
“

Kelechi

Friend, co-conspirator, family
Every adventure with you begins with a sensible plan and ends with a story no sensible person would believe. Thank you for making life larger, funnier, and more honest. Thirty looks exactly like freedom on you.
“

Aunty Nneka

Family, guide & biggest fan
I watched you become yourself one brave decision at a time. You carry your name beautifully: a princess with purpose, dignity, and a generous heart. May the next thirty years return every kindness you have planted.
